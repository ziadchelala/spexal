About Us

Spexal sarl conducts research and development projects aiming to prototype and manufacture intelligent robots for the automation of logistic processes. Headquartered in Beirut, Lebanon, Spexal works with clients both locally and overseas, designing and manufacturing products to fulfill their requirements. Spexal’s research focuses on process design, mechanical design, control and commissioning, communication, artificial intelligence, and other topics. The team consists of skilled engineers experienced in logistic processes and interfaces found in such environments. Also, highly motivated master’s and Ph.D. students work together with the team to push knowledge forward. Spexal’s goal is to provide innovative, cost-effective and robust solutions to allow for increased production and profit margin.

  • Process Design
  • Mechanical and Electrical Design
  • Software Development
  • Artificial Intelligence Development
  • Safety Implementation
  • Human-Machine Interface Development
