Difference between automation and advanced robotics

The main difference is the ability to make decision based on the current situation. A conventional automation machine performs the same predetermined movements repeaditly. An advanced robot, however, is intelligent and autonomous. It can understand its surrounding, analyse it and take action accordingly. This allows it to deal with complex real-world scenarios and solve the most challenging processes. In short, advanced robotics is a method of automation. The choice depends entirely on the process you are wishing to automate.
