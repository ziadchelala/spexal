Computer Vision Intern

Computer vision

Spexal Beirut Internship

Job Description

The position offers an opportunity to get exposed to industrial applications of computer vision technologies. Spexal is looking for a motivated and dedicated student to join the development team and be responsible for key tasks in the completion of the project.

If you are a student looking for a part time internship during your academic semester or a full-time internship during the upcoming summer, it is a chance to learn and be challenged in a growing startup tackling complex robotic and artificial intelligence subjects.

Education + Experience

  • Familiar with Linux OS.
  • Good programming skills in Python.
  • Good knowledge of processing Point Cloud and usage of Point Clouds Library (PCL).
  • Familiar with stereo vision cameras.
  • Knowledge in Robotic Operating System (ROS) is a plus.
  • Able to manage time and prioritize tasks to meet deadlines.
  • Good communication, leadership and self-learning skills.

Responsibilities

  • Perform research on advanced compter vision topics.
  • Study different types of industrial cameras.
  • Develop innovative software solution for different vision tasks.
  • Work on improving existing parts of the software.
  • Attend meetings and take minutes.

Other Benifits

  • Get to work in a highly dynamic startup environment.
  • Get involved in topics from different engineering fields.
  • Assist in all brainstorming sessions to learn and share ideas about topics in all areas.
  • Take over diverse responsibilities.

Work Conditions

  • Flexible starting date.
  • Minimum duration of 2 months.
  • Monday to Friday, 9am - 5pm.
  • Transportation covered.

How to apply

  • Send the below documents to info@spexal.com
  • Resume
  • Cover letter (optional)
